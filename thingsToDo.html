About Kasara, Maharashtra - Location Matters

Kasara is a census town in Thane district in the Indian state of Maharashtra. The town is on the busy Mumbai–Nashik route, which is one of the four major routes that lead into Mumbai.

The very first thing that one must do on a tour of this nature's paradise is exploring its natural wonders and ancient marvels. Visit Myanmar Gate, Kalsubai Peak for seeking inner resilience, or explore to learn about the city's heritage, whereas to enjoy the natural beauty, it has Camel Valley, Ashoka Waterfall several waterfalls, and much more.

Myanmar Gate

Myanmar Gate is entrance to the famous Dhamma Giri Math & Vipassana Meditation centre in Igatpuri.The gate itself is a popular tourist spot inspired from the Thailand style of architecture.It is surrounded by majestic mountains in the distance.

Kalsubai Peak

Mt. Kalsubai is highest peak of the sahyadri ranges. The view of this majestic mountain is an amazing sight in Bhandardara.It is known as the Everest of Maharashtra, as it is highest peak in the state.

Camel Valley

A few kilometers ahead of the Bhatsa river valley, lies the camel valley.It is popular picnic spot amongst nature lovers and photographers.It is best visited in the monsoon season when the area turns lush green.

Ashoka Waterfall

Located near, Vihigaon waterfall is famously known as Ashoka waterfall.A song sequence from movie Ashoka was filmed at this waterfall.It is loacted 12km from kasara.

Bhavali Dam

Bhavali Dam is a huge dam built on the river bham in the Bhavali village in Igatpuri.With a height of 111.5ft. & length of 5090ft.,the dam is visited for scenic natural beauty & is also a popular picnic spot in the region.
