The Staycation Villas ... Where Your Holiday Begins!

Our NEW and Tropical Modern Luxury Resort is nestled on Hasib-pee Hill, overlooking the ocean and KasaraBeach. The resort offers majestic panoramic city and ocean views over the andaman sea and a luxurious selection of 1 and 2 bed Villas. Built in a modern style with the finest contemporary furnishings, the resort is set in a beautiful landscaped gardens amongst the natural tropical environment of Patong's Haib-pee Hill. The Staycation Villas is the perfect hideaway destination, offering spacious rooms with all the latest conveniences as well as comfort and relaxation after your long journey. The resort offers a private location with five-star services, very close to the hustle and bustle of Phuket's popular tourist destination, KasaraBeach.

Rooms

All Villas at The Staycation Villas features a terrace or balcony with either garden or sea views and full amenities designed for your comfort and relaxation. The Resort, in KasaraPhuket, consists of 16 buildings with 383 units of which 183 units have pool access.
